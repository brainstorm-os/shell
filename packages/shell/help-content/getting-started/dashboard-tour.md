# The dashboard

The dashboard is what you see when no app is in focus. It's home base.

## The launcher

Press `Cmd+K` (macOS) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows/Linux) and start typing. The launcher searches across every app — notes, tasks, files, bookmarks, people, anything you've added. Press `Enter` to open the top hit, or use arrow keys to pick one. See [Search](../concepts/search.md) for filters and scope.

## Pins

Drag anything onto the dashboard to pin it. Pins give you one-click access to the entities you reach for most — a notes folder, today's project, an important file. Right-click a pin to remove it. See [Pinning](../concepts/pinning.md).

## The app row

Apps you've installed sit along the dock. Click one to open it. Right-click for app actions (new note, new task, etc.). New apps come from the **Marketplace** — open it from the dashboard header.

## Tray and indicators

The tray shows running apps with badges (unread, in-progress sync, etc.). Click an icon to bring its window forward; right-click to close or send to background.

## Wallpaper

Change the wallpaper from **Settings → Appearance**. Pick a bundled image, a solid colour, or upload your own.

## Settings

The gear icon in the dashboard header opens **Settings**. Vault data, appearance, [themes](../concepts/themes.md), [keyboard shortcuts](../keyboard.md), installed apps, sync — all live there.
