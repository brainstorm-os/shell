# Install an app

Brainstorm ships with the core apps — Notes, Tasks, Files, Calendar, Journal, Database, Graph, Whiteboard, Bookmarks, Code. To add more, open the **Marketplace** from the dashboard header.

## Browse and install

The Marketplace lists every app available to your vault. Each listing shows:

- What the app does, with screenshots.
- Who made it.
- What [permissions](../privacy-and-security.md) it asks for.

Click **Install** to add an app. The first time you install, Brainstorm asks you to confirm the permissions — read, write, access the network, and so on. You can deny anything you're unsure about; the app still installs but won't be able to do the things you said no to.

## Where new apps appear

After install, the app shows up in the dock and is searchable from the [launcher](./dashboard-tour.md). Right-click its dock icon for its **New** action.

## Uninstall

Open **Settings → Apps** and click the app you want to remove. Choose **Uninstall**. The app is removed, but the data it created (notes, files, tasks, etc.) stays in your vault. You can reinstall later and the data comes back.

## Themes are also installable

Themes live in the Marketplace too. Installing one adds it to **Settings → Appearance** — you can then switch between themes without uninstalling. See [Themes](../concepts/themes.md).
