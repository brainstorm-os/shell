# Welcome to Brainstorm

Brainstorm is a workspace for your notes, files, tasks, and everything else you want to keep in one place. It runs on your machine, your data lives in a folder you control, and it works offline.

You'll spend most of your time in three places:

- The **dashboard** — the home screen. It shows your pinned things, the apps you have installed, and the launcher.
- An **app** — Notes, Tasks, Files, and so on. Each app handles one shape of thing, but they share your data.
- The **launcher** — `Cmd+K` (macOS) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows/Linux). Type to jump anywhere. (`Cmd+Space` is reserved by macOS for language switching and won't reach Brainstorm.)

If you're brand new, start with [Create your first vault](./first-vault.md), then take the [dashboard tour](./dashboard-tour.md). Once you're set up, learn how [vaults](../concepts/vaults.md), [entities](../concepts/entities.md), [links](../concepts/links-and-mentions.md), and [collections](../concepts/collections.md) fit together.

Help is always one keystroke away — press `Shift+?` from anywhere.
